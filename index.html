Autobiography

Born into the noble Crownguard family, along with his younger sister Lux,
Garen knew from an early age that he would be expected to defend the
throne of Demacia with his life. His father, Pieter, was a decorated military
officer, while his aunt Tianna was Sword-Captain of the elite Dauntless Vanguard
and both were recognized and greatly respected by King Jarvan III.
